My first school

I grew up in the countryside. My kindergarten and elementary school was called Assomption. It is a small private school in the city of Saint-Dizier, France.

My journey to Paris

Soon enough, I moved to Paris. I went to the public primary school of Justin Oudin. Then I enter the Peace college and the Eugene Ionesco high school in Issy-les-Moulineaux.

A whole new world

I passed the IESEG's entrance exam in my last year of high school. Surprisingly enough, I got in. It's been a little more than half a year seen I arrived. I still don't feel like I belong there but I'm good at what I do and I enjoy myself most of the time. You must only learn to see the bright side of things and not to take yourself too seriously. But I guess that advice applies no matter your work field.

My free time

I like a lot of different stuff. I paint, I write poems, I watch lots of movies and series. Some say I'm a bit of a artist. I hardly believe that. I also love physics. I've studied it for as long as I could without any specialisation. I find it truly fascinating, especially when it's about the sky and the atoms. I'm not Bezos or Musk, I don't want to live forever or to go into space. I find beautiful enough to be satisfied with simply observing it. Don't you agree?
